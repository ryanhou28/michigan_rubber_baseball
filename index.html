Baseball is life itself.

We believe baseball is life itself: it teaches you about lessons in life - like the value of patience and perseverance, how to gracefully handle failure, and the importance of balancing teamwork with individual responsibility. It mirrors life's need for adaptability and strategic thinking, celebrates diversity, and emphasizes respect for history and tradition. Baseball also mirrors life's mix of joy and disappointment, underlining the significance of preparation, practice, understanding, and respecting rules. Most importantly, it teaches us to enjoy the journey, not just the destination.

Aspire to be like Shohei Ohtani.

Shohei Ohtani, a two-time AL MVP and a phenomenal two-way star in MLB, stands as a towering role model for us. His unparalleled skills both as a pitcher and hitter, combined with his groundbreaking achievements in the league, inspire us deeply. We aspire to emulate Shohei in every aspect, from his unwavering dedication to constant improvement to his strategic approach to the game. His ability to excel under pressure, maintain poise, and display humility in the face of immense success are qualities we admire and seek to incorporate into our own practices. In our journey, we aim to embody the essence of his work ethic, diligence, and innovative techniques.

We are lovers of baseball.

Baseball captivates us deeply; it's not just a sport, but a profound passion. The excitement of a well-hit ball, the suspense of close plays, and the game's strategic depth exhilarate us. We revere its rich history and enduring traditions, which create a bond among diverse fans. It's a source of life lessons about teamwork, resilience, and striving for excellence. Whether playing, watching, or discussing, our love for baseball is a perpetual source of joy and unity.

About Us

Rubber baseball, also known as soft baseball or nanshiki baseball (軟式野球) in Japan, is a variant of baseball played with a softer, rubber-coated ball. Sharing many of the same rules and gameplay elements as traditional baseball, rubber baseball provides a safe, inexpensive, and beginner-friendly way of playing baseball. Michigan Rubber Baseball Club is dedicated to creating a vibrant and fun environment where the UM community can enjoy rubber baseball. Students will have the opportunity to play and practice rubber baseball while building friendships, teamwork, and community. We welcome students of all skill levels, from beginners to experienced baseball players.
